void F2_layer_get_peak_height ( F2_layer_t *const  pContext,
const double_t  critical_freq_E_layer_MHz

Get F2 layer maximum density height hmF2 [km] from foE, foF2 and M3000. It is based on the method of Dudeney, 1983, J. Atmos. Terr.Phys., 45, 629-640. but modified such that the ratio foF2/foE is clipped at 1.75. Note that the clipping is soft, the 1st derivative is continuous but note the clipped value can be slightly below 1.75 at the join (but note analysis indicate > 1.73). Also, Dudeney uses a figure of 1470 rather than 1490 in the numerator of hmF2 and a figure of 1.296 rather than 1.2967 in the denominator of MF. See 2.5.5.6. hmF2
