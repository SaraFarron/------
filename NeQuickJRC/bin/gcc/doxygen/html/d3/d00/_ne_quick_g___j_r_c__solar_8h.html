angle_restricted_t solar_get_declination ( const NeQuickG_time_t *const  pTime)

Get solar declination δsun, see 2.5.4.6. The Sun's declination at any given moment is calculated by: sin(declination_sun) = sin(-23.44)sin(ecliptic_long) where ecliptic_long is the ecliptic longitude.
