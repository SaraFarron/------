static double_t get_ecliptic_longitude ( double_t  time_days)
static

The ecliptic longitude of the Sun is: L + 1.916sin(g) + 0.020sin(2g) where L is the mean longitude of the Sun L = 280.460 + 0.9856*n and g is the mean anomaly of the Sun g = 357.528 + 0.9856*n

where n is the number of days (positive or negative) since Greenwich noon, Terrestrial Time, on 1 January 2000 (J2000.0).
